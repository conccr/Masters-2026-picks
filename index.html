Competition Rules

  • Select 1 golfer from each of 6 tiers.
  • Each golfer earns +10 points per shot under par.

  • Your best 4 scores from 6 golfers count.
  • Your worst 2 scores are discarded.
  • The highest combined total wins the competition.
